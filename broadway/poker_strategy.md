# Poker Strategy

## Pot Odds

- Ratio of amount in pot to amoutn of bet to call
- Whenever odds against < pot odds, do not call; whenever pot odds > odds against, call
- Example: $70 in pot (after opponent bets 20), $20 to call; pot odds 70:20 -> 3.5:1. 37 non-clubs in deck, 9 clubs; odds against 37-9 -> just ovr 4:1. NO CALL
